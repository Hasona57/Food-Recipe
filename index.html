Savory Bacon Jam

Ingredients
  • 1 lb bacon, chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions
  1. In a large skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove bacon and drain on paper towels, reserving about 2 tbsp of bacon fat in the skillet.
  2. In the same skillet, saut√© the diced onion and minced garlic in the bacon fat until softened and golden brown.
  3. Return the cooked bacon to the skillet. Add brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer, stirring occasionally, until it thickens to a jam-like consistency, about 30-40 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Transfer the bacon jam to sterilized jars and refrigerate. Serve on toast, burgers, or as a condiment for cheese boards.

Gourmet Truffle Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients
  • 8 oz macaroni or pasta of your choice
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup truffle oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions
  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to create a roux, cooking for 2-3 minutes until lightly golden.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the shredded Gruyere, cheddar, and Parmesan cheese to the sauce, stirring until melted and smooth.
  5. Stir in the truffle oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, tossing until well coated.
  7. Serve the truffle macaroni and cheese hot, garnished with a drizzle of extra truffle oil and freshly grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
